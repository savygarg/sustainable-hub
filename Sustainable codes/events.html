~ The ASIAN Universities Summit 2023

Dr. Madhu Chitkara, esteemed Pro-Chancellor of Chitkara University,took center stage at the highly anticipated The ASIAN Universities Summit 2023. Her presence was marked by a captivating and insightful panel discussion on Sustainable Development and “Transitioning to a Clean Energy Future and the Role of Universities,” cementing Chitkara University's reputation as a pioneering institution at the forefront of sustainable innovation.


~ International Conference on Sustainable Civil Engineering Practices



Chitkara University,Himachal Pradesh in collaboration Edith Cowan University, Australia organized “Two days International conference on Sustainable Civil Engineering Practices” from 19th-20th July 2019 with the support from Department of Science and Technology, New Delhi. The 8th Annual International conference was organized with objective to embed sustainable engineering practices to meet human needs for resources,food, shelter, & effective waste management.
